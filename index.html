From crash to comeback

It started with a bike crash, and a mess of beet juice.

Back in 2012, our founder and chef Rick was searching for a better way to recover after a serious bike crash. Juicing beets was his first solution — messy, time-consuming, and far from convenient.

That challenge became Beet Boost: a simple, great-tasting powder delivering the nitric-oxide power of beets plus the recovery support of tart cherries. It now sits alongside our red spinach lineup — VitaSpinach for everyday health, RS400 for athletes competing at the highest level.

— Rick, Founder & Chef
