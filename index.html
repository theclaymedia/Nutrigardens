From crash to comeback

It started with a bike crash, and a mess of beet juice.

Back in 2012, our founder and chef Rick was searching for a better way to recover after a serious bike crash. Juicing beets was his first solution — messy, time-consuming, and far from convenient. That challenge became Beet Boost, the recovery blend that started it all.

The search for a cleaner, more concentrated source of dietary nitrate led us to red spinach — and to years of research alongside scientists at Stanford and UAB. Today that work is the foundation of VitaSpinach and RS400, the formulas we build our future on.

— Rick, Founder & Chef
